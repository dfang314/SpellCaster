These are offensive words taken from scowl. Testing of these following words (that exist in sowpods) is as follows:
shit, cunt: In profane.1, not allowed.
crap, fart: In profane.3, allowed.
kraut, wop: In offensive.2, not allowed.
Abos: In offensive.2 but capitalized, allowed. So, we are case sensitive.
n-r: In offensive.1, not allowed.
n-red: In offensive.1, allowed.
Since the above 2 conflicting words were added at the same time to scowl, there may be more research required.
